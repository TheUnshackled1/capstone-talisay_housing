Application Process

A simplified overview of the steps to apply for housing assistance through the Talisay City Housing Authority.

1

Submit Requirements

Visit the office with your documents

2

Eligibility Assessment

Staff verifies CDRRMO certification

3

Application Processing

Complete the housing application form

4

Lot Awarding

Receive notice of unit assignment

Documentary Requirements

  • Barangay Certificate of Residency
  • Barangay Certificate of Indigency
  • Cedula (Community Tax Certificate)
  • Police Clearance
  • Certificate of No Property (from Assessor's Office)
  • 2x2 ID Picture (recent photo)
  • Sketch of House Location

Important Reminders

  • Walk-in applicants are processed on a first-come, first-served (FIFO) basis.
  • Priority queue is for CDRRMO-certified danger zone applicants and landowner-endorsed ISFs.
  • The application form will only be given once ALL 7 requirements are verified complete.
  • Notarial services and engineering assessment are facilitated by THA at no cost.
  • You will receive SMS notifications when your application status changes.
