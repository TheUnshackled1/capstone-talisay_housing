Field verification workflow
Step 1 — Site inspection

Proceed to the declared address. Observe conditions, structural exposure, and proximity to the reported hazard in accordance with THA–CDRRMO coordination.

Step 2 — Record findings

Prepare objective notes and, where practicable, site photographs. Indicate whether the hazard-area claim is supported by physical evidence on the ground.

Step 3 — Lodge certification

Use Open verification on the queue row, complete the certification form, attach evidence, and submit. Module 2 staff receive the record for supervisory review.

Official notice to field personnel

• Mandate: You certify, on behalf of the Talisay City Housing Authority field unit, whether the applicant’s declared hazard-area status is supported by inspection.
• Procedure: Open the verification form from the queue, record a clear affirmative or negative certification, attach photographic evidence when available, and submit.
• Effect of record: A sustained hazard finding supports priority-queue processing; a negative finding routes the case to the general walk-in queue in line with THA rules.
• Escalation: Matters pending beyond fourteen (14) days may be administratively re-queued to protect programme integrity.
