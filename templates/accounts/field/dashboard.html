📚 Field Verification Workflow
Step 1: Visit Location

Visit the applicant's address to verify danger zone status. Check living conditions, structural safety, and proximity to hazards.

Step 2: Record Findings

Document: Is it truly a danger zone? Living conditions observed? Income level reasonable? Take photos/notes.

Step 3: Submit Assessment

Tell Jocel: "I verified this is a danger zone" or "This appears to be false alarm." Assessment feeds into CDRRMO certification.
